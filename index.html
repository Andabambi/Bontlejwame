ABOUT US

Bontlejwame Skincare is a South African skincare brand specialising in natural skincare solution that fight against common skin problem.

Our Products are Formulated using natural , safe and gentle ingredients.They are quality tested and approved to all skin types.We exist to harness the effectiveness of our African indigenous oils for cosmetics use and to give our customers the best quality experience in skin care products. Our approach to harvesting the indigenous oils is environmentally conscious with a social aim of community upliftment empowering women and youth in Africa. Social Responsibility We are very passionate about our community projects working with women and youth in economic empowerment. Our Foundation has been established and actively focuses on : Entrepreneurship, Social Responsibility, Youth Development and Mentorship.
